CoCalc OnPrem

There are various reasons why the Internet based cocalc.com service may not be sufficient for you. You may want to make use of hardware you already own or have some requirements regarding data storage and processing location.

It is possible to run a completely standalone instance of CoCalc on a Kubernetes cluster. The underlying services and their architecture are identical to those in online cocalc.com, with comparable performance, scalability, and reliability to the online site. See our store for an overview or CoCalc OnPrem documentation for detailed information.
